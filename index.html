Digital Accessibility

At PHE, we are passionate about making our digital services accessible to everyone. One this website, find resources to learn more and sign up to PHE's Digital Accessibility Community!

What is digital accessibility?

Digital accessibility means that people with disabilities can use the digital tools (e.g. a website or app) when they need to in a similar amount of time and effort as somebody who does not have a disability.

Why is this important?

  • 2 million people in the UK have some form of sight loss
  • 360,000 people are registered as blind or partially sighted
  • 13.9 million people in the UK have a disability
  • The Equality Act (2010) prohibits discrimination by disability
